About Us

Yashwant Yuva Vahini is a farmer-centric organization dedicated to empowering sugarcane farmers and rural youth. Inspired by the cooperative legacy of Maharashtra, we work to strengthen farmers through leadership, education, and innovation.

Our focus is long-term prosperity, ethical cooperatives, and youth-led rural development.

Our Programs

Sugarcane Excellence Program

Advanced farming practices to improve yield and reduce cost.

Farmer Leadership Development

Training future cooperative and rural leaders.

Agri Training Camps

Modern technology, finance, and government scheme awareness.

Market Linkage Mission

Connecting farmers directly to buyers and value chains.

Visionary leader of cooperative economics and sugar industry.

Founder – Anand Tathe

“I was inspired by Shri Sharad Pawar Saheb from an early age. His visionary leadership in agriculture, cooperative economics, and global policymaking shaped my understanding of how strong institutions can transform farmers’ lives. The cooperative legacy created by leaders like Yashwantrao Chavan, Punjabrao Deshmukh, and Sharad Pawar is not just history — it is a responsibility. Yashwant Yuva Vahini was founded to carry this responsibility forward by empowering farmers, nurturing youth leadership, and ensuring that Maharashtra’s cooperative movement remains strong, ethical, and progressive.” — Anand Tathe, Founder
