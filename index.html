About Us

VSI is a farmer-centric organization dedicated to empowering sugarcane farmers and rural youth. Inspired by the cooperative legacy of Maharashtra, we work to strengthen farmers through leadership, education, and innovation.

Our focus is long-term prosperity, ethical cooperatives, and youth-led rural development.
